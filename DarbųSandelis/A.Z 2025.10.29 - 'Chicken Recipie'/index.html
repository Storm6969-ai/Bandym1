Ingredients:

1 whole chicken (about 2 kg / 4 lb)

1-2 tbsp olive oil or melted butter

Salt and pepper

(Optional) 1 lemon, halved, and some fresh herbs (like rosemary or thyme)

Cooking:

Preheat oven to 220 °C (425 °F).

Pat the chicken dry with paper towels — moisture stops crisping.

Rub all over (and inside the cavity) with olive oil or butter, then season generously with salt and pepper.

Stuff the cavity with lemon halves and herbs if you like extra aroma.

Place chicken breast-side up in a roasting pan.

Roast for 20 min at 220 °C, then lower to 180 °C (350 °F) and continue roasting for about 1 hour, or until internal temp is 75 °C (165 °F).

Rest 10-15 min before carving — this keeps the meat juicy.

Tips:

Add flavour easily: Mix minced garlic and herbs into the butter before rubbing

Don't overcook

Let it rest uncovered (not tightly wrapped) so the skin stays crisp.
